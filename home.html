Welcome to MicroMetl

In the late 1980's plenum's were becoming obsolete by design changes to the roof top unit. Under new leadership of Jim Lindgren, Mark Webber, and Chris Schultz MicroMetl Corporation began to expand geographically. To better serve our west coast customers, a 33,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility in Sparks, NV was opened in 1994. By 1998 more space was required and the move was made across the street to a new 70,000 sq. ft. plant. In 2008 moved into a 149,000 sq.ft. in Sparks Nv. to better serve the production and engineering needs of our west coast customers. After spending 25+ years on Roosevelt Avenue in Indianapolis, MicroMetl moved its headquarters to a new 190,000 sq. ft. facility on the eastside of Indianapolis in 2000. The new plant allows for increased productivity and manufacturing capacity, engineering test labs, and larger finished good inventory levels.

Locations

Indiana

After spending 25+ years on Roosevelt Avenue in Indianapolis, MicroMetl moved its headquarters to a new 190,000 sq. ft. facility on the eastside of Indianapolis in 1998. The new plant allows for increased productivity and manufacturing capacity, engineering test labs, and larger finished good inventory levels. The Indianapolis facility provides HVAC accessories to OEM distributors across the midwest, east coast, south east, Texas, and eastern Canadian Provinces.

Nevada

Since 1994 MicroMetl Corporation has operated a second manufacturing and distribution facility in Sparks, Nevada to better serve customers in the Western United States, Canada, and Mexico. Throughout the years, the Sparks facility has twice doubled in size from its original 33,000 sq. ft. footprint to today's high-efficiency 149,000 sq. ft. plant.

Texas

MicroMetl Corp started up a new manufacturing plant in Longview Texas in 2005. Longview's primary focus is to provide quality products and services to the commercial HVAC plants in the Midwest and southern U.S. The Longview plant is over 100,000 sq. ft and is equipped with the latest technology in both the machining and designing of products. ISO 9001:2008 certification was obtained in June 2006.
